Fluffy Pancakes

Description

Recipe for making fluffy pancakes! This recipe will yield 4 servings.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1 cup all-pupose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • cooking spray

Steps

  1. Combine milk with vinegar in a medium bowl and set aside for 5 minutes to "sour"
  2. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and baking soda, and salt in a large mixing bowl. Whisk egg and butter into "soured" milk. Pour the flour misture in to the wet ingredients and whisk until lumps are gone.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium heat, and coat with cooking spray. Pour 1/4 cupfuls of batter onto the skillet, and cook until bubbles appear on the surface. Flip with a spatula, and cook until browned on the other side.
